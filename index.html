Artificial Intelligence (AI)

AI or Artificial Intelligence, refers to computer systems capable of performing tasks that typically require human intelligence, such as learning, problem-solving, and decision-making. It involves creating machines that can simulate human intelligence processes, like learning from data, recognizing objects, and understanding language.

Advantages of AI

  • Faster Decision-Making
  • Automation
  • Unbiased Decisions
  • Improved Safety and Security
  • Reduced errors
